Trust Us

Health To Go is the platform that you can trust with the interpreter and the hospital and we provide a great service to contact for the international hospital

Enter your diagnosis and select your preferred treatment method in the search You can also select a country and a city (optional). if you cannot find your diagnosis or treatment method,use a feedback system and We will assist you.

We allowed you to select the hospitals and contact to the popular medical travel destinations that Cambodians prefer such as Thailand, Vietnam, Singapore, and many more.

Our App are selected based on research as to the facilities, proximity to major cities, ratings, reviews, and so many more.

Our interpreters are skilled in translating technical medical terms directly into Khmer Language, so you no longer have to worry about Googling doctor instructions or diagnosis.

If the program you have selected matches your diagnosis the consultant confirms your program and you get a set of payment documents from financial department. The program may be amended at your request.

After payment, you will receive a voucher (booking confirmation) which you must present to the coordinator of the chosen hospitals or clinics upon arrival. You will also receive a detailed information letter which informs you about the plan of your medical tour.
