More details:

Centrally located in the heart of Bangkok, Bumrungrad International Hospital is one of Thailand's premier hospitals. It offers its patients one-stop medical care in Asia's and Thailand's first JCI (Joint Commission International) accredited hospital. The Hospital is JCI accredited since 2002, the first hospital in Asia to be DNV GL MIR Certified for Infection Risk in 2017, and the first non-USA hospital to receive the Global Healthcare Accreditation with Excellence designation from GHA - a reflection of the highest international quality and safety standards for overseas patients going to the hospital for treatment and care.

Established on 17 September 1980, the Bumrungrad International Hospital, Bangkok is a global pioneer in giving the best international standard healthcare services.

As the largest private hospital in Southeast Asia, Bumrungrad International has over 700 highly qualified physicians and dentists. There are over 554 inpatient beds and Bumrungrad has the capacity to treat 3,000 outpatients per day.

The hospital provides ongoing health care services for patients and families anytime, anywhere.

Services include consultation with physicians, certain types of laboratory tests, and getting medicines.
